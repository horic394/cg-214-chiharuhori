Iceland, a mysterious country

Iceland is one of the mysterious countries in the world. I guess it is because "Ice" prevents people from going there. Also Icelandic artist, Bjork globally delivers mysterious atmosphere through her music. Iceland is a tiny but very powerful and interesting country. It's capital city is Reykyavik.

 

The entire population is almost 330,000.Especially, one-third of population lives in Reykjavik.Also, a half of the national population live neighboring cities around Reykjavik.Only Akreyri is a northern city which is the forth largest city in Iceland.
