About the blogger

Hi! I'm Chiharu! I'm a Graphic Designer / Visual Artist from Japan currently based in NYC. I grew up in Shizuoka, Japan which offers scenic ocean landscapes and of course, hot springs! My adventurous and inquisitive spirit brought me to challenging cities such as Tokyo, Reykjavik and New York. While living in Iceland, I visited many European countries because my friends from same Japanese univerisity also studied abroad. Meeting up with them as well as local people were good excuse to excape from the cold icy land for a while. After Iceland, I visited North American continent to see my ex-boyfriend who studied in San Diego and met up with old roomies who studied together in Iceland. I love Asian countries as well! Good food, charming local people and cool cultures.

My future goal is to visit South American continent and Iran. I definately should research about if I can access to local bathing cultures! And I hope the day that I can travel anywhere without any danger, comes soon!
